# HHCC - UI Design Mockups & Wireframes

> Angular 18+ wireframes mapped to the 12 Business Use Cases.
> Color Palette: Primary `#dd2c00` (Red), Accent `#fbc02d` (Gold), Info `#0288d1` (Blue), Success `#388e3c` (Green)

---

## Screen Map Overview

| # | Use Case | Angular Route | Component Location |
|---|----------|--------------|-------------------|
| 1 | Home Screen | `/` | `app/home/` |
| 2 | User Registration | `/register` | `app/profile/` |
| 3 | Add Family Member | `/profile/family/add` | `app/profile/` |
| 4 | Manage Family Members | `/profile/family` | `app/profile/` |
| 5 | Add Pet | `/profile/pets/add` | `app/profile/` |
| 6 | Manage Pets | `/profile/pets` | `app/profile/` |
| 7 | Schedule Pick-Up/Drop-Off (Family) | `/scheduling/family` | `app/scheduling/` |
| 8 | Schedule Pick-Up/Drop-Off (Pets) | `/scheduling/pets` | `app/scheduling/` |
| 9 | Make Payment | `/payment` | `app/payment/` |
| 10 | Email Notification | (System - No UI) | N/A |
| 11 | Feedback & Support | `/support` | `app/support/` |
| 12 | Admin Dashboard | `/admin` | `app/admin/` |
